Research experience

Face recognition and verification

Advisor: Jiwen Lu | Department of Automation | Tsinghua University

With the advanced network architectures and the discriminative learning approaches, deep CNNs have boosted the face recognition(FR) performance to unprecedent level. An A-Softmax loss for CNNs was proposed to learning discriminative face features with clear geometric interpretation, which intrinsically matches the prior that faces lie on a spherical manifold. In this project, I mainly evaluated the Sphereface model on the Magaface challenge dataset.

Template adaptation evaluation for IJBA dataset

Advisor: Jiwen Lu | Department of Automation | Tsinghua University

Traditional evaluation methods of face recognition mainly focused on one-to-one verification. For the IJB-A face recognition dataset, a template adaptation method was proposed to compare different templates and achieve higher face recognition performance. I mainly evaluated the performance of Sphereface with this templated adaptation on IJB-A face dataset.

[report][code]

Disentangled representation GAN for Pose-invariant face recognition

Advisor: Jiwen Lu | Department of Automation | Tsinghua University
A disentangled representation generative adversarial network(DR-GAN) was proposed to learn pose-invariant feature from face images. We were interested in reproduce the performance of DR-GAN and tried to improve it on the feature fusion part.

[report][code]

Gesture recognition based on CSI

Advisor: Yuan He | Department of Software | Tsinghua University

This project aims at detecting and recognizing human gestures based on WIFI channal signal information. We used a PCA and dwt based feature extraction method to extract discriminate feature from the 30 dimensional WIFI signal, and then we trained a SVM classifier to recognize the specific 9 gestures.
